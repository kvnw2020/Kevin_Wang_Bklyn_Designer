LightScan was developed as a byproduct of an exploratory side project with a Product Manager and Developer, focused on the LangChain framework. LangChain empowers developers to create text-understanding and text-generating AI applications. I was responsible for designing the product and ensuring a seamless user experience, while my team expressed interest in exploring this backend technology.

We embarked on a project with the purpose of harnessing the power of AI to tackle a particular issue. Through in-depth discussions and thorough research, we arrived at the realization that students could greatly benefit from the integration of an AI-powered chrome extension. It is a common occurrence for students to encounter research anxiety and feel overwhelmed by the vast array of academic journal articles available in online library databases. Our primary objective was to create a tool infused with AI capabilities, enabling the streamlining of the research process for students. By facilitating the discovery of pertinent articles and offering concise summaries along with essential insights, we aimed to enhance comprehension and alleviate the challenges students often face.

After an 8-week agile sprint, our team of three successfully launched our MVP product on the Chrome Web Store. While we were delighted with our accomplishment, our work is not yet complete. We are currently in the process of updating the extension's design and also considering the implementation of a PDF upload feature.
